Team India T20 World Cup 2024

The felicitation ceremony of the T20 World Cup-winning Indian cricket team at Mumbai's Wankhede Stadium has concluded. Earlier, a T20 World Cup victory parade of Rohit Sharma and co. kicked off at Marine Drive and concluded at the iconic Wankhde Stadium, crossing a sea of fans. The crowd was jam-packed as the Team India bus tried to find its way to the destination. The likes of Virat Kohli, Rohit Sharma, Suryakumar Yadav and ex-coach Rahul Dravid found it hard to keep their emotions under control As the victory bus slowly and gradually made its way through the massive crowd.

T20 World Cup victory celebration continues

The players distributed balls to the fans. They sent it into the crowd with the help of tennis racquets.What follows is some lucky fans taking selfies and asking for autographs from the cricketers. The players are now walking off the ground.
